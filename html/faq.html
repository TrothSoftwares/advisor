    • What is Demat account?

  • Dematerialisation is a process whereby an investor's holding of investments in physical form (paper), is converted in to digital record.In order to avail this facility,the investor needs to open a demat account with a depository participant.

    • What is SIP(Systematic Investment Plan)?

  • SIP is a process where investors invest consistent amount at periodic intervals.

    • What is SWP(Systematic Withdrawal Plan)?

  • SWP is a process in which the investor offer of re-purchase constant value of unit over a period of time.

    • What is STP(Systematic Transfer Plan)?

  • In STP the amount that withdrawn from the scheme by the investor is re-invested in other scheme of the same Mutual Fund.
